Privacy Policy for Talk to Semantic Scholar

Introduction
Welcome to the Privacy Policy for the "Talk to Semantic Scholar" GPT. This policy is designed to inform users about our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information (if any) when you use our service. 
The primary function of our GPT is to facilitate access to academic papers and scholarly research from the Semantic Scholar database without collecting personal data from users.

No Personal Data Collection
We do not collect any personal data from users of the "Talk to Semantic Scholar" GPT. Our service is designed to provide information and facilitate research without the need to collect, store, or process personal information from our users.

Usage Data
The GPT does not store or log any usage data that can be personally identifiable. Interactions with the GPT are designed to be informational and are not tracked or recorded for the purpose of identifying users or their preferences.

Third-Party Services
Our GPT utilizes publicly accessible data from the Semantic Scholar database. We do not control the privacy practices of Semantic Scholar or any third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information about their data practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We advise you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
